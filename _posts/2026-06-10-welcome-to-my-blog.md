This is the first post on my blog. I built this site to keep practical
engineering notes in one place — things I learn, problems I solve, and ideas
worth writing down.

<!--more-->

## Why write things down

Writing forces clarity. If I can't explain a solution in a few paragraphs, I
probably don't understand it well enough yet. A blog is just a public version
of that discipline.

## What to expect

- Short, practical posts focused on real problems.
- Backend, systems, and tooling topics.
- No clickbait, no fluff — just notes that I'd want to read myself.

Thanks for stopping by.
